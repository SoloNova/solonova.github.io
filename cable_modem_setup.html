Cable Modem Setup

With todays technology it is easy to have any device that is capable of connecting to the internet using only ONE Internet Service Provider connection. SoloNova's Computer Solutions can help you setup existing computer to the internet along with setting up new computers to exsisting networks.

Here’s what we’ll do:

Evaluation:

Prior to beginning the installation, we’ll make sure all of your components are compatible with one another and if any new hardware is required we'll let you know prior to any work being done and make sure you understand exactly what work will be performed.

High Speed Cable Modem Setup:

We’ll setup Cable Modem or xDSL router and setup all computers that were identified to be connected to the shared internet.

Validation:

Once the initial setup is complete, we’ll verify that all of your devices are connecting properly.

Demonstration and Questions:

We’ll demonstrate how to get the most out of your new Shared Internet Solution, answer any questions you may have, and make sure you’re completely satisfied with our service.
